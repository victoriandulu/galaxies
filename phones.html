Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose landline phones over mobile phones?

Landline phones offer superior reliability, especially during emergencies and power outages. They provide better call quality, enhanced security, and in many business settings, they're essential for multi-line capability, conference features, and specialized communication needs.

How long does a typical installation take?

Installation time varies depending on the complexity of your system. A standard residential installation typically takes 2-4 hours, while business systems with multiple lines and extensions may require 1-2 days. We always provide a time estimate before beginning work.

Can you integrate landlines with my existing communication systems?

Yes! We specialize in integrating landline systems with VoIP services, intercom systems, and existing network infrastructure. Our technicians can create a seamless communication ecosystem that combines the reliability of landlines with modern technology.

Do you provide maintenance services after installation?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive maintenance packages to ensure your landline system continues operating at peak performance. Our service plans include regular check-ups, emergency repairs, and system upgrades as needed.

What types of landline systems do you install?

We install various landline systems including traditional analog lines, digital PBX systems, VoIP-enabled landlines, multi-line business systems, and specialized communication networks for different industries. During our consultation, we'll recommend the best option for your specific needs.
