2019

Our Beginning

In 2019, a group of young and passionate software developers came together with a vision to innovate and create solutions for the tech industry. Committed to fostering inclusivity and empowerment, they have since prioritized mentorship programs and job opportunities for youth and young women, aiming to bridge the gender gap in technology and provide valuable career pathways for the next generation of tech leaders.

2021

Expanding into Hardware Services

By 2021, we broadened our scope by introducing specialized hardware services to meet the growing needs of our clients. These services included CCTV installation, network data cabling and data points setup, Starlink internet installation, alarm system installation, server setup, and office phone system installation. Our commitment to delivering quality solutions established us as a trusted partner for businesses looking to enhance their technological infrastructure.

2024

Our Impact

Today, we are a leading software development company, impacting businesses across the globe with our cutting-edge solutions. In line with our commitment to fostering inclusivity and empowerment, we offer affordable tech lessons to young people, creating job opportunities and bridging the gender gap in technology. Our initiatives align with global efforts to promote employment opportunities for youth and women through vocational and technical training.
