Frequently Asked Questions

How secure are biometric door systems?

Biometric door systems offer significantly higher security than traditional keys or access cards because they rely on unique physical characteristics that cannot be lost, stolen, or shared. Our systems also include anti-spoofing technology to detect fake fingerprints or photos, and all biometric data is encrypted using military-grade encryption standards.

Can biometric systems be integrated with our existing security infrastructure?

Yes, our biometric door systems are designed for seamless integration with most existing security systems, including alarm systems, CCTV networks, fire safety systems, and building management software. We support standard protocols like Wiegand, OSDP, and API integrations to ensure compatibility with your current infrastructure.

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies depending on the size and complexity of your facility. A simple single-door system can be installed in a few hours, while a comprehensive enterprise solution for multiple locations might take several days. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations, and we can schedule installations during off-hours if needed.

What happens during a power outage?

Our biometric door systems can be configured with battery backup systems that provide several hours of operation during power outages. Additionally, you can set doors to fail-safe (unlocked during power failure) or fail-secure (locked during power failure) depending on your security requirements and local fire safety regulations.

How is biometric data stored and protected?

We take data protection very seriously. Biometric data is stored as encrypted templates, not actual images of fingerprints or faces. These templates cannot be reversed to recreate the original biometric data. All data transmission is encrypted using AES-256 encryption, and our systems comply with relevant data protection regulations including GDPR where applicable.

Do you offer maintenance and support services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages tailored to your needs. These include regular preventive maintenance, emergency technical support, software updates, and rapid response for hardware issues. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist with any issues that may arise.
