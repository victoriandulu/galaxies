Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to develop a custom software?

Development time varies based on project complexity. Simple systems take 2-4 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do you offer post-deployment support?

Yes! We provide comprehensive maintenance and support services including bug fixes, updates, security patches, and feature enhancements. Support packages are available based on your needs.

Can you customize existing systems?

Absolutely! We can customize and enhance existing software systems, integrate new features, and optimize performance based on your evolving business needs.

What are your payment terms?

We typically work with milestone-based payments: initial deposit at project start, progress payments at key milestones, and final payment upon delivery. Custom payment plans are available for larger projects.

Do you work with clients outside Kenya?

Yes! While we're based in Nairobi, we serve clients across Kenya and internationally. We use modern communication tools to ensure seamless collaboration regardless of location.
